Biggest Choke of the Century!

Atlanta Falcons Blow Their 28-3 Lead in Superbowl 51 to the New England Patriots!

Severall exquisite memes and photos in response to this joke of a performance

Superbowl 51, hosted in Houston, Texas, was won by the New England Patriots in overtime against the Atlanta Falcons. With two minutes and twelve seconds left on the game clock in the third quarter, the Patriots started the biggest comeback in Superbowl history, scoring an unanswered 31 points through the rest of the game, all the way into overtime. The final play was a two yard touchdown rush in overtime by James White, a running back for the Patriots, winning them their 5th Championship. This superbowl left many football fans shocked at the what would become some of the most iconic scores in Superbowl history, and in doing so, caused many memes and remarks about the falcons, and their remarkable and unique to ability to choke.
