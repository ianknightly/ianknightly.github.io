Biggest Choke of the Century!

Atlanta Falcons Blow Their 28-3 Lead in Superbowl 51 to the New England Patriots!

Severall exquisite memes and photos in response to this joke of a performance



Superbowl 51, hosted in Houston, Texas, was won by the New England Patriots in overtime against the Atlanta Falcons. With two minutes and twelve seconds left on the game clock in the third quarter, the Patriots started the biggest comeback in Superbowl history, scoring an unanswered 31 points through the rest of the game, all the way into overtime. The final play was a two yard touchdown rush in overtime by James White, a running back for the Patriots, winning them their 5th Championship. This superbowl left many football fans shocked at the what would become some of the most iconic scores in Superbowl history, and in doing so, caused many memes and remarks about the falcons, and their remarkable and unique to ability to choke. The final score of the Superbowl 51 was 34-28.



This first image is a meme off twitter. 2016 was a big year in terms of losing leads in sports championships. Not only the the Atlanta Falcons blow their 28-3 lead over the New England Patriots, but both the World Series and the NBA Finals were also cases of teams choking. Both the Cleveland Indians and the Golden State Warriors lost their 3-1 game leads over their respective opponents. This meme refers to how the Falcons could somehow pull off a bigger lost lead than the Warriors.



Coincendetly to the Falcons blowing a 28-3 lead, the Patriots Superbowl 51 rings, had 283 diamonds, yet another troll of Atlanta.



This second image is another post on social media, with the intention of trolling Matt Ryan, the Atlanta Falcons' quaterback in Superbowl 51.



Unforntunatley for the Falcons, the trolling was not limited to social media. Stores like Walmart, for example, put up scores of the game as pricing discounts for Atlanta Falcons merchandise.



Sadly (for the Falcons), Patriot fans weren't the only fans trolling them for a while about their Superbowl performance. Between the other teams in the Falcons division and those teams fans, everyone in the NFC South proceeded to make fun of the Falcons in one way or another.
